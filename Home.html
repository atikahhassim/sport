The Mukah Restaurant

A Classic Restaurant with a Modern Approach

The Blue Leopard is the place to come for a relaxed kampung atmosphere with modern decor. Our drinks list that boasts a large range of Mukah food selection. We've been informed that our attic is haunted so in honor of our supernatural guests, we have a Watch Party every Saturday night from 7pm until close. Live music seven nights a week, and never a cover charge for entry.
